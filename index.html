Our method

Measured, then lived with.

Every rating blends objective measurements — frequency response, distortion, output power — with weeks of real listening and the long memory of the wider community. We take no payment for placement. The only thing on sale here is honest advice.

01

No sponsors decide the order

Rankings owe nothing to affiliates. Retailer links are listed for convenience only, grouped by region so you can buy from someone you trust.

02

Synergy matters

Budget gear shines when paired well — a warm headphone with a clean amp, a neutral IEM with flexible EQ. Each review flags what to pair it with.

03

Prices are honest estimates

Street prices in USD, accurate to mid-2026 and prone to drift with coupons. Always double-check the current price before you buy.
